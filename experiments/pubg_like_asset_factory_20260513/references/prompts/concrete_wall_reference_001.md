# Concrete Wall Reference Prompt 001

Purpose: realistic material reference for `concrete_wall_material_v1`.

Prompt:

```text
Seamless realistic dirty concrete wall material for rainy tactical checkpoint, subtle cracks, chipped paint, grime streaks, water stains, rough aggregate detail, bullet impact marks as decal candidates, neutral overcast lighting, flat front-on material reference, PBR texture generation target.
```

Negative prompt:

```text
cartoon, stylized, clean plaster, heavy perspective, text, labels, repeating obvious pattern, glossy plastic
```

Acceptance:

- suitable for basecolor/normal/roughness/AO generation;
- tileable or easy to crop into tileable material;
- close-up readable roughness and normal variation.
